About Us

AZURE GLOBAL began in 2003 to cater to the increasing demand for quality residential and commercial development in Canada and China. Our expertise is in a wide range of real estate development activities; these include land assessment, land acquisition, planning, architectural designing, construction, and effective various types of project management. The company’s main aim is to acquire and develop quality properties which are a profitable investment while being also appreciated for its design, comfort and practicality.

Each of our projects is executed after a thorough market research to fully understand our client’s preferences and tastes. We try and accommodate our client’s every request during the design phase. Our planning, attention to detail, and excellent designs are evident in all our land and real estate development projects.

Mission & Vision

Vision

To be the ideal Global Company operating in multiple sectors in the Real Estate, Developments and Construction industry by providing our clients with value driven solutions to meet their needs for sustainable development.

Mission

As a globally recognized company, we will serve selected industry sectors across multiple geographic locations. Guided by our relentless focus for success for our business, clients and stakeholders, we will deliver excellence exceeding our commitments.

Core Values

Integrity

We operate in an intensely competitive environment, we compete fairly, conducting our business in a professional manner that reflects favorably on our company and on each of us.

Respect

We are sincere, fair and forthright, treating others with dignity and respecting their individual differences, feelings and contributions.

Accountability

We set high performance expectations and hold ourselves accountable for the quality of our work and the results we achieve as individuals, as team members and as a company.

Entrepreneurship

We encourage non-traditional thinking and appropriate risk-taking, which provide opportunities to learn and grow as individuals and as businesses.
